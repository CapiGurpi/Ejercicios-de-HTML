Edson Barboza's life
Edson Barboza is a Brazilian mixed martial artist known for his explosive striking and devastating leg kicks. Born on January 21, 1986, in Nova Friburgo, Brazil, he competes in the UFC’s featherweight division. A former Muay Thai fighter, Barboza gained fame for his speed, precision, and highlight-reel knockouts—most notably his spinning wheel kick KO over Terry Etim. He’s respected as one of the most technical and dangerous strikers in UFC history.
